## Section 5: The LinkedIn Research Task

**"Find one person doing what you want to do in 10 years."**

This is not a hypothetical exercise. Do it now or complete it before the next time you revisit these notes.

---

**Step 1: Define the target role**

In 10 years, I want to be a _______________ at a _______________ type of company.

(Examples: "Senior ML Engineer at a fintech startup" / "Quant Researcher at a proprietary trading firm" / "Staff Backend Engineer at a product company" / "Data Scientist at a sports analytics firm")

---

**Step 2: Search on LinkedIn**

Search terms to try:
- "[Role title] NITW" — find alumni in that role
- "[Role title] NIT Warangal" — same search, different keyword
- "[Company you want to work at] NIT Warangal" — find alumni at that specific company
- "[Role title] India [city]" — broaden if you cannot find NIT alumni

---

**Step 4: The insight question**

What is the single most useful thing you learned from looking at this person's profile?

---

**Optional step:** Connect with them on LinkedIn with a short, specific message. Not "I want to be like you" — something like "I'm a student at NITW M&C starting in [year], I found your profile while researching careers in [field], and I'd love to ask one specific question about [X] if you have 10 minutes." Most people do not respond. Some do. One response from the right person can change your path.
