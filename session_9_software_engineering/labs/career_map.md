## Part 3 — The Job Description Test

Look up **two real software engineering job descriptions** on LinkedIn or Naukri.com.  
One entry-level (0–2 years), one mid-level (3–5 years).

For each job, identify:
1. Which era's skills are **required**?
2. Which era's skills are **nice to have**?
3. Is AI/LLM experience mentioned? How?

Write 4–6 sentences comparing the two job descriptions.

## Part 4 — Discussion with Sree

Work through these questions together:

1. **"If you were a software engineer in 2010, what would your hardest problem be?"**  
   *(Think: no cloud, no containers, no CI/CD, deploy by copying files)*

2. **"Microservices solved scaling. What new problems did they create?"**  
   *(Think: 700 services, who owns each? How do you test them together?)*

3. **"Platform engineering builds golden paths. Who benefits most from this?"**  
   *(Think: junior engineers joining a new team)*

4. **"AI copilots write code faster. Does that mean software engineers are being replaced?"**  
   *(Think: what parts of engineering can AI NOT do yet?)*
